Hi, I'm Jake.
I'm a computer engineer.

So... who am I?

I am a Senior at the University of Akron. I am majoring in Computer Engineering and minoring in Computer Science and Mathematics. I live and breathe in code. When I was 9, I created my own video game. At age 12, I started teaching myself C++. Now, as a 22 year old living in Akron, OH, I am preparing to apply my skills that I have learned to help others accomplish their dreams.

What sorts of things have I done?

  • Web Development

    If you need someone to create or edit your website, I'm your guy. I bend HTML, CSS, and jQuery to their limits while I generate elegant, glowing code and a smooth user experience. With allies such as Photoshop and Dreamweaver, my design abilities know no end.

  • Application Development

    I have written countless applications for both mobile and desktop platforms, including everything from simple number conversions to complex hardware control systems. I am passionate about each end every line of code that I write, and have spent many of my days perfecting my algorithms to behave quickly and accuratly. With tools like C++, Perl, and MATLAB, I can create anything that I can imagine.

  • Hardware Design

    Hardware systems go together with code like bread and butter. Whether it's designing a simple filter or creating an entire embedded system from stratch, I slave over the soldering iron until my circuits work flawlessly.
